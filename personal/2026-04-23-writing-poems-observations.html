Poem — Kara
Kara is so huggable So very very loveable Everytime I see her She really needs a wee her Bladders uncontrollable Her ears are somewhat foldable I love that her tummy is moldable Bum like a bubble but double Her chest is the best to rest when stressed I'd endure trips and nips to kiss those lips Smells like a scented candle With a temper too fiery to handle Best thing to do is to make her a brew Rub her back and tell her a joke or two Hold the tomato, skip the egg Promise I'll not mention the beef leg The most delicate of creatures Hard to find all these features In one person in life So I made her my wife Need you when nearer Miss you when farrar Love you lots Beautiful Kara
