12 August 2024 Personal

If you were an animal, which one would you be?

A swan — because I am serene on top and paddling furiously underneath where no one can see my efforts.

Would you rather fight one horse-sized duck or 100 duck-sized horses?

A horse-sized duck would be dangerous — its bill could kill you and its wings might knock you over. I'd rather fight the duck-sized horses. Although 100-to-1 odds are terrible, they couldn't all attack at once because they'd get in each other's way.
