Comic / short story structure

Writing notes

Comic structure: beginning, middle, end. Six parts — setup, introduce the problem, anti-climax obstacle, more challenges, problem solved, resolution. Character arcs important: how does the character change? Starting idea: a man with 3 brothers who aren't talking. Main character is just playing with his kids.

Story format — Pixar template

20 Sep 2016

Once upon a time _____. Every day, _____. One day _____. Because of that, _____. Because of that, _____. Until finally, _____.

By phrasing a sales pitch (or story) this way, it automatically becomes a narrative — buyers should always be the hero of a sales story.
