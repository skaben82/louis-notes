NOTES INBOX
===========
Drop any note in here from any device. Claude will process this when you ask.

HOW TO ADD A NOTE:
------------------
Start each note with a line like this:

---
DATE: 10 May 2026
AREA: work
NOTE:
[your note here — typed or pasted transcript, as messy as you like]

---
DATE: 10 May 2026
AREA: personal
NOTE:
[next note]

Areas: work / personal / content / side-hustle
If you're not sure which area, just write your best guess — Claude will check before filing.

When you're ready to process, just tell Claude: "process my inbox"
Claude will clean everything up, create the HTML notes, flag actions, and clear this file.
