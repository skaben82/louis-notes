Breakfast — Makes 5

Ingredients

1kg Four Berry Medley (£2.99) · 240g Porridge Oats (£0.90) · 2kg Fat Free Natural Yoghurt · 250ml UHT Skimmed Milk · 1.5 tbsp cinnamon

Overnight Oats (4 portions)

  1. Combine 40g oats and 50ml milk (or water) into each container and press down to form a base.
  2. In a large bowl mix 1.6kg FFNY, cinnamon, 800g berries, and optional vanilla extract until fully combined.
  3. Divide into 4 and spoon over each oat base.
  4. Lid on, pop in the fridge overnight.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

Protein Oats (1 portion)

  1. Combine 40g oats, 50ml milk, and 100ml water in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. After 4 minutes, add 200g frozen berries.
  3. Once berries are incorporated, add 400g natural yoghurt.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

Meal Prep 1 — Slow Cooker Cheesy Fajita Chicken Pasta (makes 6)

Ingredients

300g penne pasta · 1 packet fajita cooking mix · 900g chicken thighs · 100g grated mozzarella · 1 tin chopped tomatoes · 2 onions (sliced) · 3 peppers (sliced) · 200ml water · Optional: 3 tsp lazy garlic + 3 tsp lazy chilli

Method

  1. Add chicken, onions, peppers, tomatoes, garlic, chilli, fajita seasoning and 200ml water to slow cooker. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika. Stir well.
  2. Cook on high for 4 hours or low for 6–8 hours.
  3. Remove lid, stir, then add pasta and 100ml more water. Cook on high for 25 minutes.
  4. Top with mozzarella, cook on high a further 5 minutes until melted.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
