General outreach (formal)

Subject: Is Your Building Fully Compliant with UK Fire Safety Regulations?

Dear [Client's Name],

At eBanks Fire Protection, we understand that the safety of your people and property is paramount — and staying compliant with UK fire safety regulations is not just good practice, it's a legal requirement.

A thorough Fire Risk Assessment (FRA) is the first vital step in protecting your building, ensuring you meet the latest standards under the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005. Our experienced team can help you identify potential fire hazards, recommend practical actions, and provide clear documentation for compliance and peace of mind.

If you'd like to book a no-obligation consultation, please get in touch.

Kind regards,
eBanks Fire Protection
