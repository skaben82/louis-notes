One stormy night, a man was out walking his favourite pet, Gordon the goat.
He headed into the woods towards the wood cutters cottage, but oh no, what was that he stood on, he wondered?
Alas, Gordon was dead, his neck snapped by the man's size 12 boot. 'Shucks, she will not be happy' said the man.
The man loved Gordon. He was his best friend. Even though he had only three legs he was a prize winning goat. The man trudged home back to his wife Imelda to break the bad news over some chicken fried steak.
Tears overflowed as the man thought of his accidental act of savagery. But as he trudged back down the a34, slowly his frown turned upside down and became a smile. He remembered all the good times, he and gordo shared. The time when...
They decided to shave off all their hair. What a night that was thought the man, a teensy bit chilling on the nips but boy they had some chuckles. The man continued in this thought as he reached home only to find...
Baubles. Lots of shiny baubles. Red ones, blue ones, glittery ones. His wife had been busy decorating for crimbo and the house was a kaleidoscope of colours. 'Bernadettarina, I have something to tell you
Although dear sweet Gordon has pegged it, we can have oodles of curry goat for Christmas, he declares to his left foot (for the man, you see, is not quite the shilling). His wife smiles at her husband and ushers him into..
The downstairs bathroom. 'There's a spider in there sort it out , babes. I've been waiting for you to get back for ages.'

The end
